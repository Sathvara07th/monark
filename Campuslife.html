Campus Life

Monark University Arts & Culture programs enrich the lives of the students & faculty as well as enhance learning through arts and culture on campus and in the community. We host an array of culture events throughout the year. From classical music, paintings and dance to jazz, comedy skits and sculptures. We have incredible talents for every taste and member of the campus and community. We are also commemorating various Patriotic Events and historic days in our campus like Sovereign Republic, Independence Day and so on. We always raised patriotic fervor in the crowd and revived the oath of being dutiful children to this country.

NCC

University runs actively and dedicatedly NCC Programs for the students and always encourage them be a part of NCC. We provide the appropriate environment to develop discipline, leadership and responsibility among the students. Our cadets are actively participated in various camps like RDC, TSC, SNIC, AITC, EBSB, Army Attachment Camp, Inter Group Camp, etc.
